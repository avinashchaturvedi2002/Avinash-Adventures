Cycling

We embarked on an exhilarating 90km cycling journey from MANIT to the historic Bhimbetka rock shelters. The ride started with crisp morning air and rolling terrains that tested both my endurance and spirit. Along the way, I was greeted by lush greenery, serene villages, and the occasional chirping of birds. Reaching Bhimbetka, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, was immensely rewarding, as I got to witness the ancient rock paintings and immerse myself in history. Rock Climbing

Tenzing Rock, nestled in the scenic beauty of Darjeeling, offered me an unforgettable rock-climbing adventure. Named after the legendary Tenzing Norgay, this natural rock formation is a dream for thrill-seekers. As I geared up, the adrenaline surged, and the challenge ahead felt both daunting and exciting. With each ascent, the rough texture of the rock tested my strength and determination, but the panoramic view from the top was worth every effort. The experience was a perfect blend of physical endurance and mental focus, leaving me with a deep sense of accomplishment and a renewed love for the mountains.

Waterfalls

Nestled near the serene Nandi Hills, the enchanting Chunchi Falls is a hidden gem that captivated me with its natural beauty. Surrounded by rocky landscapes and lush greenery, the cascading waters create a soothing melody that instantly calms the soul. The trek to the falls added an adventurous touch to the journey, as winding paths and scenic views accompanied me along the way. The cool mist from the waterfall was refreshing, and the tranquil atmosphere made it a perfect spot to unwind and connect with nature. Chunchi Falls is truly a must-visit for anyone exploring the Nandi Hills region.

Hiking

The hike to Zhiri Waterfall, located near Bhopal, was an exhilarating escape into nature's tranquility. The trail, surrounded by dense greenery and winding pathways, offered a refreshing break from city life. As I ventured closer, the sound of cascading water grew louder, adding to the excitement. Upon reaching the waterfall, I was greeted by the breathtaking sight of water tumbling down rocky cliffs into a serene pool. The cool mist and the peaceful ambiance made it a perfect spot to relax and soak in the beauty of the surroundings, making it an unforgettable adventure near Bhopal.
